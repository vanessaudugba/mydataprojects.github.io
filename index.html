GreenLeaf Stores
Mangement Dashboard

GreenLeafStores is an early-stage online retailer, based in the United States of America, specializing in eco-friendly home products. In just its first year, the startup has seen rapid order growth but still relies on gut instinct and ad-hoc reports rather than a consistent, data-driven approach.

For this project, I used Microsoft Excel to analyze GreenLeaf Storesâ€™ operational and sales data, transforming ad-hoc information into structured insights. The analysis supported a shift from intuition-based decision-making to a more consistent, data-driven approach, helping the business better understand performance trends and support informed strategic decisions.

The GreenLeaf Stores Management Dashboard provides a clear view of retail performance, highlighting revenue, profit, orders, and units sold. Built in Excel, it analyzes sales and profitability by product and state, tracks key KPIs, and visualizes trends, top customers, and regions showcasing strong data modeling, pivot tables, and dashboarding skills.
